Our Mission

Axobiome is a microbiome biotechnology startup focused on developing probiotic and microbiome-based therapeutics for people with cystic fibrosis (CF). We aim to restore microbial balance, improve gut and lung health, and support better clinical outcomes through science-driven innovation.

What We Do

We combine microbiome research, genomics, and translational science to design targeted probiotic strategies for CF-associated dysbiosis.

Why It Matters

People with CF often experience chronic antibiotic exposure and microbial imbalance. By developing rationally designed probiotics, Axobiome seeks to support healthier microbial communities and improve quality of life for CF patients.
