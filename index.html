Our Mission

Axobiome develops microbiome-based therapeutics for cystic fibrosis. We aim to restore microbial balance, improve gut and lung health, and support better clinical outcomes through science-driven innovation.

What We Do

We combine microbiome research, genomics, and translational science to design targeted probiotic strategies for CF-associated dysbiosis.

Why It Matters

Chronic antibiotic exposure in CF disrupts microbial communities. Rationally designed probiotics can help rebuild healthier ecosystems and improve quality of life.
